What is Osmotic Pressure?

  • It’s a colligative property — depends on number of dissolved particles
  • Pressure needed to stop water flow across a semipermeable membrane
  • Plant cells use it to keep that juicy turgor 😎
Define the four colligative props and connect to root water movement.

Hydro Systems: Why OP Matters

  • Ions in solution ↑ OP → changes root water uptake
  • Too low → underfed; too high → osmotic stress / tip burn
  • Sweet spot = consistent growth & quality
Mention yield & cannabinoid profile consequences when OP drifts.

Manage the Solution

  • EC by stage: 0.8–1.3 seedling • 1.2–1.6 veg • 1.6–2.0 flower
  • Mass balance Replace transpired water with proper nutrient strength

Yield & Quality

  • OP & salinity can nudge THC/terpenes
  • Future: AI + strain-specific recipes

Takeaways

  • OP = the root’s water gatekeeper
  • Dial ion strength, not just pH
  • Monitor EC • Manage mass flow • Prefer closed loops
